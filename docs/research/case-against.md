# The Case Against

## 1. The product that works is the product students won't use.
- Deslauriers et al. (2019, PNAS, Harvard): Active learning scored 0.46 SD higher but students reported 0.56 SD lower "feeling of learning." Preferred the passive lecturer.
- Karpicke et al. (2009): 84% use rereading as main strategy. 1% use self-testing. Product forces self-testing -- fighting 99% of market default behavior.

## 2. Education apps have worst retention in mobile.
- Day 30 retention: 2-3%
- Average customer lifespan: ~4 months
- Duolingo loses half by Day 7 with hundreds of engineers on retention
- 90% of education apps deleted within 30 days

## 3. Students cram. Product requires sustained use.
- 25-50% of students cram (one study: 64%)
- Med school Anki: active users dropped from 48 to 10 across one year (79% decline)
- Only 11% of Anki users were heavy users
- ~50% of SRS app users use them for cramming, defeating the purpose
